Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Scope

This policy applies to mobile apps published by Little Lanterns Cove unless an app has its own app-specific policy.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal data directly from you.

Depending on the app and features you use, data may be stored on your device, such as settings, favorites, custom names, and other local app state. In Baby Bracket, this includes items like favorites, custom names, settings, and a local entitlement cache.

Purchases

In-app purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store and StoreKit. Little Lanterns Cove does not run its own payment processing systems for in-app purchases.

iCloud / Device Sync (if used)

Some apps may offer optional sync through Apple iCloud, tied to your Apple account, so data can be shared across your devices.

If iCloud sync is disabled, or if a specific app does not support it, data stays local to your device. In Baby Bracket, iCloud sync is optional for supported data, while entitlement cache stays on-device.

Analytics / Ads / Tracking

We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, ad SDKs, or third-party tracking SDKs in our apps at this time.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are designed for simple family use. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Parents and guardians should review app settings and device controls to decide what is appropriate for their family.

Data Deletion and User Controls

You can remove locally stored app data using in-app reset or clear options when available, or by deleting the app from your device.

If iCloud sync is used, synced data can be managed through Apple iCloud settings for your account.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date on this page.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to hello@littlelanternscove.com.
